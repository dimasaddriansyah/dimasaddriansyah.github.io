Problem Statment

People in Indonesia have been longing for a very interesting and enjoyable vacation. There are several factors that need to be considered so that there is no need to worry about where to go, where to stay, whether the place to be visited is in line with expectations or not, and most importantly whether it is in line with the budget available. Often, people choose their vacation destinations from friends, relatives, or family members who have previously visited the tourist destinations. Because they have gained experience from them before, they have no doubt in choosing the tourist destination, even though they don't know if it meets their expectations.

Possible Solution

The purpose of creating the Tour & Travel application is to make it easier for the community, especially in Indonesia, to make tourism and hotel reservations quickly and easily, as well as to provide recommendations for tourist destinations and hotels. The result achieved in creating the Tour & Travel application is a mobile-based digital platform that can meet users' needs without fear of not meeting their expectations and provide recommendations for tourist destinations that match their desires.

User Flow

The Userflow is designed to understand the travel process that is directed by the user, starting from selecting a destination, choosing a hotel, selecting an agent, making payment, and receiving a booking confirmation. The Userflow starts from the onboarding page, then proceeds to the login page. If the user does not have an account, they can create one on the registration page. Once the user is logged into the application, they will choose the destination, hotel, or agent and desired travel dates. After that, the user will make the payment. Finally, the user will receive a booking confirmation via email or the application. The Userflow must be well-designed and easy to understand so that users can make bookings easily and without difficulty.
